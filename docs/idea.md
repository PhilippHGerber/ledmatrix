# LEDMatrix

**Create retro LED signs in seconds.**

Turn any text into realistic highway signs, flip-dot displays, and neon boards. Export as image or animation – perfect for social media.

* LED Matrix

## Features

- Authentic LED matrix look with glow effects
- 4 color themes (Amber, Neon, Warning Red...)
- Export as PNG
- uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, punctuation marks, and umlauts
